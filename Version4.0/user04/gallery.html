Lexus LFA

The 2012 Lexus LFA is a 2-seater V10-powered supercar made by Toyota's luxury division, Lexus. It was a hand-built and perfected car with only 500 units ever made, boasting 553 horsepower. Toyota lost money on every model they made, but it has gone down in history as a legend and one of the greatest cars ever made.

C6 Corvette Z06

The C6 Corvette is the sixth generation of corvette, made from 2005-2013. The Z06 model is the performance model, with a 7.0L V8 making 505 horsepower to the rear wheels.

BMW E30 M3

The BMW E30 generation was made from 1986-1991, with the M3 being the highest performance model. The E30 M3 won more than 1400 races and numerous championship titles in almost every conceivable series right up to the world championship. This makes it still the most successful touring car in the world today.
